Burglar

A VR robbery simulation with horror elements. Break into homes, liberate valuables, and escape with your life using standard VR interactions and custom systems.

# Overview

This was a group game project where programmers, artists, and designers collaborated. As an aspiring thief, you must navigate hazardous environments, solve puzzles, and avoid detection.

# My Contributions

Player Systems

  • Implemented PS VR 2 controls (movement, snap rotation).
  • Collision system preventing players from walking through walls while allowing head movement in constrained spaces.
  • Dynamic height calibration based on headset position.

Interaction Systems

  • Physics-based grabbing with drop thresholds.
  • Hybrid interaction system supporting both free-grab and snap-to-position objects.
  • Component-based interaction architecture for modular actor behaviors.
