Full Moon Fears

Play as a werewolf fleeing persecution. Avoid detection by your former friends and neighbors in this stealth-horror experience where you cannot fight back.

# Overview

In this group project, you are a newly transformed werewolf who has tragically killed his family. Devastated, you attempt to flee the valley, but the townsfolk are hunting you. You must escape without harming anyone.

AI Architecture

Idle & Patrol

Enemies wait or move between points. Sighting the player triggers other states.

Chase & Attack

Direct pursuit and engagement when in range. Attacks check for weapon overlap.

Search

Investigates last known player position upon losing line of sight.

Coordination

Enemies call for backup immediately upon spotting the player.

Enemy Manager

Class responsible for spawning and destroying enemies based on player proximity triggers, optimizing performance by managing active entities.
